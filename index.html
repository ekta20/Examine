About Us


Examine helps you to carry out your vision and hearing ability test at home for free.

With our strategically guided tests for both vision and hearing we provide you with not only reliable results but also a hassle-free and quality experience. And all this from the comfort of your own house.

The tests carried out at examine are also available for download so that you can have a good record of your medical history and can share it with your doctor and family. So, take a test with our built-in tool and get a step closer to a healthy living.
